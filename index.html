Hsieh Kai-Hsiang   謝凱翔

My name is Hsieh Kai-Hsiang. I completed my B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Chung Yuan Christian University in 2023, under the guidance of Prof. Li-Der Jeng. During my undergraduate studies, I undertook a research project titled “Application of YOLO for Drug Identification System”, which was submitted to the Workshop on Consumer Electronics (WCE 2022). In this project, I applied the YOLO object detection framework to develop a system capable of accurately identifying drugs. This experience gave me hands-on skills in computer vision and deep learning, and practical system implementation. It laid a solid foundation for my future research.

After graduation, I pursued my M.S. in Electrical Engineering at National Chung Cheng University, which I completed in 2025 under the supervision of Prof. Jui-Chiu Chiang. My master’s research focuses on machine learning, computer vision, video processing, and point cloud compression. My thesis, titled “End-to-End Joint Compression Framework for Static Point Clouds Using a Single Encoder and Dual Decoder,” integrates deep learning-based point cloud compression with a multi-directional Mamba backbone, enabling the model to capture rich spatial and channel-wise dependencies while maintaining linear-time decoding and accurate entropy modeling. This work has been accepted to the IEEE/CVF WACV 2026 international conference.
